About me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Zhejiang University, working under the supervision of Prof. Jiming Chen and Prof. Qi Ye. Prior to this, I earned my PhD in Computer Science from the Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, where I was advised by Prof. Zenglin Xu. During my doctoral studies, I conducted a research internship at Huawei Noah's Ark Lab (Beijing), focusing on perception technologies for autonomous driving locomotion systems. Prior to joining the Ph.D. program, I obtained my Master degree from Nagoya University, supervised by Prof. Fujii. My undergraduate education was completed at Harbin Institute of Technology, Weihai, a scenic seaside city in North China.
